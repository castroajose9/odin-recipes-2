Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a delicious recipes that will take you back to your mother recipes and homecooking dish. A classic of Italian cuisine

Ingredients

  • Meat : 1/2 pound of lean ground beef of your choosing 85% lean
  • Onions : one whlie diced tomatoes
  • Canned tomatoes :
  • Fresh Herbs : two tablespoons of parsley and crush one clove of garlic
  • Spices and Seasoning : dried basil, dried oregano, salt and pepper
  • Noodles : Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese : Parmesan and shredded Mozzeralla
  • Eggs : 2 Eggs

Instructions

  1. Cook the meat :Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly. Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent. Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  2. Cook the Noodles :Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they're al dente.
  3. Make the Cheese Layer :Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Assemble the lasanga : Layer the ingredients according to the recipe (starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled.
  5. Bake the lasagna Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about half an hour. Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is gliden brown.
